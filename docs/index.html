Everything a player should be

One app for the whole library you actually own.

Server and phone, blended into one. Wrapped in a player that's fast, gorgeous, and completely yours.

Your library, everywhere

Stream from your own Subsonic / Navidrome server, and blend in the MP3s on your phone and SD card — one seamless collection in search, shuffle, and browsing.

9 living themes

Midnight, Aurora, Sunset, Ocean, AMOLED and more — plus colors pulled straight from the album art you're playing.

World radio

Thousands of live internet stations. Hit “surprise me” and land somewhere new — Lagos, Reykjavík, anywhere.

Synced lyrics & chords

Time-synced, auto-scrolling lyrics that follow the song — plus one-tap guitar chords.

Made to feel effortless

Swipe to change tracks, swipe up for the album, drag to reorder your queue, multi-select to build a mix, favourites, offline caching, sleep timer — and it resumes exactly where you left off.

Private by design

Nothing leaves your network. No account, no sign-up, no tracking, no profiling. Your listening stays between you and your own server.

The first truly private music AI

An AI that runs on your machine.

Point Sonora at your own Ollama server and it becomes a music companion — building queues from a vibe, keeping the music going, and teaching you about what's playing. It never phones home to a cloud.

  • 🎧
    AI DJ & Smart Radio — “upbeat 90s for the gym” becomes a queue from your library, and it keeps refilling itself.
  • 📖
    Grounded insights — facts pulled from Wikipedia so the AI tells you real history, not hallucinations.
  • 🔮
    Discovery — find similar songs; if it's in your library you play it, if not you preview it in-app.
  • 🌍
    Translate & explain lyrics, ask anything about a track, or just talk to it with your voice.
